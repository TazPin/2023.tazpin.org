Exciting Days

Welcome to TazPin 2023

TazPin is a public exhibition of pinball machines from all eras.
It is a chance to discover the silver ball for the first time, or to relive fond memories of the past.

When

Saturday + Sunday; 29 - 30 July 2023

Where

Australian Italian Club Of Launceston
414 Westbury Rd, Prospect Vale TAS 7250

What

50+ Pinball machines on free play!

All profits go to charity 🔗

Last year $4,000 donated to the 4k Children's Ward LGH.

All the pinball you can get

Featuring classic machines from a bygone era, all the way up to modern marvels and just released titles.

Single entry: $30

Children: $10

Under 16. (Must be accompanied by a ticketed adult).

Family pass: $60

2x Adults + 2x Children. ($10 per additional child).
